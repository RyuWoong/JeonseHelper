# Information Architecture

## Purpose

JeonseHelper is a question-tree guide for people who are new to jeonse and for tenants who suspect jeonse fraud. The product should help users understand their current situation, choose a response path, run the right applications or legal procedures, and recover after major outcomes such as guaranty insurance payment, victim recognition, or auction completion.

This is not a general blog. The left navigation is the source of truth, and page content should be filled under these navigation entries.

## Primary Audience

- Tenants learning jeonse for the first time
- Tenants who suspect fraud or face deposit return risk
- Tenants who need to apply for public support, guaranty insurance payment, or court procedures
- Tenants preparing a safer next lease after a difficult experience

## Chapter Roles

`1. 피해 대응` helps users find their position first: suspicion, diagnosis, guaranty insurance status, and senior or junior priority risk.

`2. 신청과 법적 대응` contains procedural manuals for applications, claims, court filings, auctions, distribution, enforcement, and long-term recovery.

`3. 피해 회복` starts after meaningful outcomes happen. It covers what to check after guaranty insurance payment, victim recognition, auction completion, partial recovery, and housing or life stabilization.

`4. 다음 집은 안전하게` is the practical prevention chapter for the next lease or renewal.

`5. 서류와 자료` is a reference workspace for documents, agencies, case numbers, consultation notes, evidence, and official links.
